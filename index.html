🌱Plant Care Tips

  • Open box immediately after receiving.
  • Air dry plants for 2–3 hours.
  • Use soil mix: 30% sand + 30% soil + 20% vermicompost + 20% perlite.
  • Water after 2–3 days.
  • No direct sunlight for 10 days for new succulents.
  • Water only when top soil is dry (check roots, not leaves).
